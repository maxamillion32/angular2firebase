Vietnam's first local delivery

We are pleased to introduce Price Fishbone Vietnam's first local project delivery, the Generali Vietnam. Generali Vietnam is a newcomer among insurance companies in Vietnam which provides life insurance and they were looking for a provider that could help them to implement a system of Image Content Management, that take care of scanned applications of life insurance, including management of all attachments etc needed in the process. We won the contract in competition with two suppliers from India and one from Israel, they offered Alfresco and IBM FileNet. Delivery has been running since November.
